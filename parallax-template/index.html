Battlefield Hardline Review:

    Multiplayer is amazing new modes are a much needed refresher to the franchise. I have found 3 new modes as my favorite. Rescue blood money and Hotwire. The old Battlefield game modes are there but seem, well, old. Still fun but I find myself playing completely different modes. Hardcore mode has been up and working from Day one. My only criticisms are that while the money gun unlocking system is amazing some weapons cost a lot and you can only use selected weapons as a good guy or bad guy. The solution is getting money (easily done by playing hotwire) but still its a little tedious.
    Single player: best shooter single player I have ever played. Feels like your playing episodes of your favorite kick ass cop show, but you can choose to shoot any one of those fools breaking the law. Though you are rewarded well for playing like a cop and arresting rather than shooting people (sorry keith) you can discover new evidence while in missions to solve extra crimes and arrest suspects with warrants for bonus xp.
    All in all I say buy it. I am not buying premium yet and will probably do dlc as it comes. If anything jump on a sale when the first one comes. Oh and they nerffed snipers, its no where near as bad as it used to be. Im a little pissed about it but it means even on hardcore you have to hit a chest or above shot for a 1 shot kill
